Free Fire


Description :

The game begins with the collection of about fifty unarmed players in one place and then transported by plane to a remote island, free firehouse where the player is allowed to choose where he prefers to jump from the plane and use the parachute to land on the island to start the arms race, when you fall you will be unarmed Completely, you have to look for weapons and gear inside the houses on this island, you can also search for vehicles and use them in transport, or run over other players, if you are a fan of the challenge, choose a crowded area with players to start the game, either if you want to focus on survival, choose de more Quiet. In the game there are two distinct areas, the red zone and the safe area, the safe area at the beginning of the game include the entire island, and then after some time the game will give you a warning message that the safe zone will shrink, and here you have to move to stay inside, because staying outside it means decreasing your points of life Gradually to death, so you have to remain vigilant towards the changes of this region, as this region will continue to be bothered until the last survivors are forced to confront among themselves to identify the lone survivor, so as to solutions without wasting time in searching for a needle in a haystack, the second area is the red zone, which Move within the safe area and you have to avoid it, because falling within it will expose your site to the enemies and thus pose a danger to you for being an easy target. The game continues until only one person survives. You can play as a team or individually, where team members can communicate with each other by voice through the microphone, and they can also offer first aid if someone is almost dead.
